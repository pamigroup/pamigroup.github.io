desp: Bob Zhang is currently an Associate Professor in the Department of Computer and Information Science at the University of Macau. He obtained his B.A. in Computer Science from York University in 2006, a M.A.Sc. in Information Systems Security from Concordia University in 2007, and a Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University of Waterloo in 2011. After graduating from Waterloo he remained with the Center for Pattern Recognition and Machine Intelligence, and later worked as a Post Doctoral Researcher in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Carnegie Mellon University. His research interests focus on biometrics, CV, pattern recognition, image processing, ML, AI, etc.
